Work packages
Outputs of the project

Key deliverables of IONO-DIET.

IONO-DIET will produce a set of scientific, methodological and operational deliverables, including software, catalogues, publications and training of young researchers.

Together, these deliverables define the long-term legacy of the project. :contentReference[oaicite:24]{index=24}

Main deliverables

Scientific, technical and societal outputs

Online catalogue of natural hazarddriven disturbances

A publicly accessible catalogue of ionospheric disturbances associated with earthquakes, tsunamis and other natural hazards, based on space and ground observations collected and analysed during the project. :contentReference[oaicite:25]{index=25}

IONO-DIET NRT package & prototype

A near-real-time processing package and prototype demonstration that process GNSS data streams, detect ionospheric signatures of natural hazards and infer associated parameters. This includes codes capable of handling multi-GNSS data in real-time. :contentReference[oaicite:26]{index=26}

Methods to distinguish ionospheric disturbances

New methods that distinguish natural hazarddriven ionospheric disturbances from those caused by geomagnetic storms, meteorological processes and other sources, with a focus on NRT applicability. :contentReference[oaicite:27]{index=27}

Scientific publications & training

Articles, theses and reports

Peer-reviewed articles

The project foresees at least 13 research articles, with contributions from both the French and Brazilian teams, dealing with LOAI coupling, NRT methods and applications to early warning. :contentReference[oaicite:28]{index=28}

Doctoral theses & student involvement

IONO-DIET will support a dedicated PhD thesis funded by the ANR and will contribute to at least two additional PhD theses linked to the project, as well as training of master students. :contentReference[oaicite:29]{index=29}

Reports & scientific meetings

Regular scientific reports and presentations at international symposia will document the progress and results of the project, as summarised in the Gantt chart of outcomes. :contentReference[oaicite:30]{index=30}

Software & web services

Long-term technical legacy

TEC and NRT processing software

Upgraded TEC processing chains and NRT algorithms will be made available to partners and, where possible, to the broader community, facilitating further research and potential operational use. :contentReference[oaicite:31]{index=31}

Web page and online tools

A project web page and online tools will serve as a hub for documentation, data visualisation and dissemination of the online catalogue and demonstrator results. :contentReference[oaicite:32]{index=32}
